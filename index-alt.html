Reactor Labs


An Incubator focusing on advanced Internet-Of-Things startups

Reactor brings innovative hardware startups from Tel Aviv- to the heart of Silicon Valley. We invest in bright, early, promising entrepreneurs, and by leveraging our deep knowledge and network of Silicon Valley, we make them fundable by Silicon Valley capital.

We assembled a dream team of Internet-of-Things and Hardware experts, from companies such as Apple, Samsung, Google and Intel. Under their guidance, we combine efficient Tel Aviv execution with Silicon Valley go-to-market know-how to create amazing companies.

Core Value

  • We are involved

    We not only invests dollars, but also work closely with founding teams to help discover market fit. With our deep expert network, we help to define a tight and investment worthy product and the traction targets for seed round

  • We are Internet-of-Things

    With a clear focus and Identity, we stand out with investors and entrepreneurs. We add tremendous value to startups, due to our expertise and industry connections. And as the Internet is expanding into the physical world, we know that Internet-of-things represents a big opportunity that would fuel many great companies in the next decade and beyond

  • Your door to Silicon Valley

    Soft landing into the best innovation ecosystem in the world. Through our deep network, we open doors into Venture Capital and Business Partners, allowing entrepreneurs to immerse themselves in the thriving culture and form partnerships that would build their companies long term viability

What makes us different?

Unlike so many accelerators, we don't "mentor" or provide you with an office. We are a virtual founder in your team, involved in stratgey and execution toward a Silicon Valley seed round.



We are not "just" an accelerator "program"
more about how we work

  • Talent

    We believe in finding talent early and overseas. We look for pre-seed high quality teams that can pivot to meet opportunities.

    We invest in people first, space second, idea is subject to pivots and is used to validate the team.

    70% team +25% the-space +5% idea

  • Focused Space

    We focus on opportunities in a hot and productive space - Advanced Internet-Of-Things.

    We believe that smart connected devices are the next big thing for the internet, and were our experience, expertise and contacts can add value to startups.

  • Focused Value

    We focus on preparing the startup for a Bay Area seed round.

    This translates to two major steps. The first is to tune the idea into something the market will respond to.

    The second is traction that earns the seed round from from a Bay Area Venture Capital firm.


Focus on early stage team and opportunity

  • Invest in a team that has what it takes to win in Silicon Valley

  • First money into the company (pre-seed / seed)

  • Look for an opportunity where both market and potential investors are in the United States

Domain expertise and execution support

  • Focus on advanced IoT (includes IoT, wearables, robotics, deep learning and other enabling technologies), both Business-to-business and Business-to-consumer

  • Hands-on strategic involvement and incubation

  • Deep network of solution experts for hardware, IoT, marketing and productization

Silicon Valley go-to-market know-how

  • Negotiate a vision that aligns with Silicon Valley core beliefs

  • Definition of key milestones and traction metrics - critical factor in readiness for first significant round (Seed or A)

  • Refine pitch and match startup with investors and partners
