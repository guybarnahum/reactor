How We Work

What we do

for startups

The most important thing is work on the idea and its market fit. We iterate with founders until we feel we have something great to build. We make sure startups are incorporated properly and are investment ready. Then we invest.

We especially interested in web leveraged IoT / Consumer Technology as we spent long time in the space and can add value.

We help founders deal with investors. This starts early, planning the right traction targets that would position the company for a seed round. We advise on many aspects of development, intellectual property strategy, etc.

We pre-flight the goals and pitch with friendly investors feedback to the team. We teach founders how to pitch. Once there is traction we locate and introduce the right investors to the company and are there when the deal is forming.

We don't "mentor"

We facilitate execution that is tuned to achieve a US seed round readiness.

In the Concept phase, we work with the founders to match their idea to the market.
Define who is the customer and map key goals to make the venture investment friendly.
In the Planning phase, we define specific traction goals for the round, derive organization and technology architecture, advise on technology selection, and track progress.

Although typical timeframes are 6 months in Tel Aviv for concept, planning and traction, these phases may bleed into each other, or even loop. In parallel, a 3 month period of Investment Planning and Pitch Development are done in San Francisco.

Founders are expected to move to the Bay Area for the Seed round. The startup should have a location strategy that make sense for investors.
